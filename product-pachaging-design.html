Product Packaging Design Examples

meat brand & spices brand

For this meat brand, I made two packaging designs for them. They are applying to different retail shops. The target audience of left-side design is young people, for this design the business owner uses it on the online shop and supermarkets which target young people. Right-side of the design, they use it on traditional market and special campaigns like Lunar new year campaign, etc.

Most packaging designs for spices in Taiwan are old-fashioned, so I tried to make a hipster design for this spices brand. Using sketch elements and handwriting front to create their packaging.
