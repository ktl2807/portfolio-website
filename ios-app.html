iOS App

Closet Management App

This idea comes from myself and my friends who have lots of clothes. What bothers us is we have too many clothes and can’t remember every clothing in our closet. So when we try to mix and match the outfit or buy new clothing we want to make sure we won’t purchase similar items or we have clothing that can match it.

On the homepage, I made a simple list that users can add or find their items easily. An easy form on the clothing page in which users can add details about clothing, like materials, washing instructions, and pictures.
